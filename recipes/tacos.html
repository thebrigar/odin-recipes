Slow Cooker Chicken Tacos

Home

Description

Slow Cooker Chicken Tacos are a convenient and flavorful meal made by cooking chicken in a slow cooker with taco seasoning and salsa, then shredding the chicken and serving it in tortillas with your favorite toppings. The dish is known for its ease of preparation and versatility, allowing for customization with various seasonings and toppings.

Ingredients

  • Broth
  • Taco seasoning
  • Chicken

Steps

  1. Season the Chicken
  2. Place it in a slow cooker and pour the broth over the chicken
  3. Cook on low for 6 to 8 hours
  4. Shred the chicken
  5. Spoon chicken into warmed corn tortillas
  6. Garnish with shredded lettuce, chopped tomatoes, and thinly sliced red onions, if desired
